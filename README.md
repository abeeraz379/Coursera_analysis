# exploring_and_analysis
1. Which course difficulty level has the highest total enrollment, and approximately how many enrollments does it have?
      Answer: Beginner level has the highest total enrollment, with around 40 million enrollments.
2. Which course organization has the highest average course rating, and what is that rating?
      Answer: The organization with the highest average rating is shown in the bar chart, with an average rating near 5.0 (the exact highest bar in the chart).
3. What is the total enrollment for the course with the highest enrollment compared to the one with the lowest?
      Answer: The highest enrolled course has about 80.68 million enrollments, while the lowest has around 396,000 enrollments.
4. Which course title has the highest enrollment among those listed?
      Answer: Machine Learning has the highest enrollment among the listed course titles.
5. Which type of certificate (COURSE, SPECIALIZE, or PROFESSIONAL) has the highest enrollment, and what is its percentage share?
      Answer: COURSE certificates have the highest enrollment, making up 63.37% of all enrollments.
6. What is the maximum course rating possible, and what is the average course rating across organizations?
      Answer: The maximum course rating is 5.00, while the average rating across organizations is around 4.68.
7. How does the enrollment for SPECIALIZE certificate programs compare to PROFESSIONAL certificate programs in terms of total numbers?
      Answer: SPECIALIZE programs have significantly higher enrollment (27.26 million) compared to PROFESSIONAL programs (2.29 million).
